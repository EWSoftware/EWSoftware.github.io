      Remarks

      The conversion to string breaks the duration down by largest to smallest unit (years down to seconds). This version allows you to specify the maximum unit of time that should be represented. Any time in excess of the maximum time unit will be returned in the maximum time unit (i.e. PT1D will be returned as P24H for a maximum time unit of hours, P1M2W5D will be returned as P7W for a maximum time unit of weeks if a month is defined as 30 days).
